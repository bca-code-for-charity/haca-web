Haitian Alliance for Community Action Inc.

Creating the basis for strong families and successful communities.

Our Mission:

To create better life opportunities for the most underprivileged children in the underdeveloped rural communities of Bellevue de la Montagne, an area south of Petionville, Haiti.

To provide them with the basic necessities of life such as food, education, and health care in order to facilitate the development of individuals into productive citizens, creating the basis for strong families and successful communities.
